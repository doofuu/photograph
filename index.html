With yet another year gone by,
Of it being eventful, I can testify.
Come with me and I shall tell
What ensued, in a nutshell.

Explorations galore
In good ol' Bangalore; 
The directions of the compass, 
Uncovered by our very own doofus.

Galloping in the midst of greenery
In Lalbagh and Cubbon Park, enjoying the scenery; 
In JP Park, christening an innocent passerby, "aunty";
In Police Traffic Park, just falling short of sanskaari.

Hoping, at Thubarahalli Lake, not to get abducted; 
At Kaikondrahalli Lake, where yoga was disrupted. 
Sshh! Pelicans made Madiwala Lake seem so serene, 
while Jesus water turned Ulsoor Lake dark green.

Ah! It will be unbecoming of me to ignore
Sneaking undercover in through the door
Of Diamond District, White Houzz and the Leela Palace Hotel,
And Ritz Carlton and Royal Orchid as well.

Besides St. John's, with deer, getting acquainted; 
Evicted from St. Francis Xavier's for not being sacred; 
Propaganda in the Bangalore Palace under disguise; 
Following a Doggo to a shelter, gazing into their cute eyes.

Not to forget the air of competition, 
Defeated, in air hockey and snooker, by a narrow margin,
Only to be brought, in carrom, to my knees
And "boggle"d by Birdie's quick ABC's.

Reading habits grew by leaps and a bound, 
As BYOB, we unexpectedly found.  
New houses we got to explore,
along with IIM, JP Nagar and so much more.










Spellbound by Handlebards and Ruskin bond's short skit, 
And a rendition of Kafka's work, totally unfit,
We discovered at NGMA, Films from a bygone age
Permitted us to conclude that all the world's a stage.

At Ambedkar Bhavan and Cubbon park, rhythms and a solo show; 
Musical tastes expanded, listening to BBC radio. 
"Unforgettable" is the way in which we sang Despacito. 
Once sworn to be truly non-eeettt, but no mo(re).

Of course, fascinating tales of inebriation
Cannot have the bad fortune of being left forgotten - 
From sprinkling oregano in happy-hours in Bootlegger
To riding a witch's broom and transforming into a wine taster.

Of Knowledge, never did cease, our pursuit -
Cycling around and exploring science at the Tata Institute; 
Artificial Life with Zinadine Zidane and Kaafi with Kuriosity; 
In and around the planetarium, playing with gravity and water's velocity.

Bitten by bugs of wanderlust and contemplation,
On New Year's Eve, camping beneath the stars, in a grassland-y destination;
Long drives to God's own country, a picturesque hill-station,
Moonlight talks and hugs beside a coffee plantation.

When, to Whitefield, my immigration, inevitable, proved to be,
Fears filled my heart, of the future, being dreary.
But through it all, I realized how much I could rely
On this girl who is a supercalifragilisticexpialidocious ally!

Conversations covering every sort of topic, 
Ranging from the microscopic to the macroscopic,
Now we discuss everything under the "moon", 
Our multitudinous whatsapp groups would make Zuckerberg swoon.

Of the version upgrades, 'tis certain I must commend! 
Oh! Through 11 levels did our protagonist ascend!
Winning a hackathon and nanodegree,
A year ago, not even the Doctor could foresee.
From actually reading books completely to publishing on Medium,
One could now certainly claim Birdie believes in "Carpe Diem".













Our symbiotic relationship, I love most certainly, 
For better people, it makes us most assuredly,
Our "I, winner" contests must never cease,
As we strive together, to be a masterpiece!

Happy Twenty-Four!
Here's looking forward to version upgrades galore!
Yet another revolution around the Sun!
Oi Doofus! Have lots of fun!
